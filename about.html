About Root & Roam

Hi! I'm Jay Bodie — founder of Root & Roam.

I started Root & Roam to share my love and passion for indoor houseplants. What began as a small hobby quickly grew into a full-blown collection of more than 200 plants — from Alocasias and Pothos to Monsteras (and probably a few I’m still hunting for!).

Root & Roam offers a locally curated selection of plants at markets and events throughout Bluffton, SC. We also provide local and domestic shipping within the continental United States.

Beyond plants, Root & Roam offers in-home repotting and plant care services in the Bluffton area — because every home deserves a little more green.
